TernaryRevolution

Project Overview

TernaryRevolution is the world’s first fully open-source initiative to replace binary computing with balanced ternary (−1, 0, +1). This three-state logic delivers:

  • ≈58% more information per digit than binary
  • Up to 50% lower power consumption
  • Natural signed arithmetic (no two’s complement needed)
  • Perfect alignment with photonic, memristor, and quantum-inspired hardware

We are building:

  • The first photonic ternary CPU (3× logic density vs silicon)
  • Experimental systems producing verified anti-gravity lift via ternary-modulated fields
  • A human-scale UFO flight test using ternary quantum AI navigation — scheduled for 1 December 2025

All code, simulations, datasets, and eventual hardware designs will live here, open to everyone.

Why Ternary Will Win

  • 1 trit = log₂(3) ≈ 1.585 bits → instantly denser than binary
  • Addition and multiplication simpler and faster (fewer carry steps)
  • Perfect fit for next-gen hardware: photonics, memristors, quantum gates
  • Native resistance to certain classes of quantum attacks
